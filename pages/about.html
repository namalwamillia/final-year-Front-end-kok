About Us

King of Kings Multi-Investments & Consultancy Farm Limited is a registered agribusiness dealing in the production, processing, commodity aggregation, and marketing of oil seeds like sunflower, sesame, and soya alongside cassava as a food security crop.

Our Mission

To drive business action to sustainably enhance and scale up capacity and contributions of farmers towards Food & Agriculture with focus on Climate Change Adaptation, value addition and E-marketing of safe, quality, nutritious and affordable diets by 2030.

Our Vision

To evolve into company with enough capacity to drive business action toward Climate Change, Food and Nutrition for inclusive growth and decent Job creation.

Our Achievements

Tractor awarded by Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fishery in 2021.

Awarded by PSFU/UNDP in 2021 among the best companies in championing gender equality, 6th position.

Recognized for excellence in agro-processing in 2022.
